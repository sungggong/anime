### Short description
AniPick is a fast anime recommendation web app that turns your current mood, genre preference, story focus, pace, and available watch time into a ranked anime pick with a clear one-line plot summary and a simple reason why it fits.

### Product description
AniPick helps anime fans decide what to watch next without endlessly scrolling through streaming catalogs.

Take a short taste quiz, choose the kind of mood or story you want tonight, and AniPick ranks matching anime from a curated catalog of 200 popular titles. Each recommendation includes a real one-line plot summary, matching percentage, and a quick reason so users can decide fast.

It is designed for casual viewers, returning anime fans, and anyone who wants a low-friction recommendation experience before starting a show.

### Maker comment
Hi Product Hunt! 👋

I built AniPick for the exact moment when you want to watch anime but spend more time scrolling than choosing.

Instead of only filtering by genre, AniPick asks what kind of experience you want tonight — action, comfort, romance, mystery, fantasy, fast pacing, emotional tone, story focus, and watch length — then gives you a ranked recommendation with:

- a match percentage
- a real one-line plot summary
- why the pick fits your taste
- a 200-title anime catalog
- a simple watchlist shelf

The goal is simple: help you choose your next anime in under 30 seconds.

I would love feedback from anime fans, indie makers, and recommendation-system nerds: what should AniPick add next?
